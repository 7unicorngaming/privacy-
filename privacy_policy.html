Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2025

1 · Who we are

This policy applies to the Facebook/Instagram integration called “7unicorngaming”, operated by 7unicorngaming.

2 · What data we process

  • Basic profile information you authorise Facebook to share with us (name, e-mail, profile picture)
  • Content you choose to schedule for posting (text, images, videos, captions, etc.)

We do not request or receive any other categories of Facebook data.

3 · Why we process your data

We access the data solely to schedule and publish the content you ask us to post. We do not use it for advertising, analytics beyond your own account, or any unrelated purpose.

4 · Storage & Security

  • No persistent storage: Your data resides only in temporary server memory while the post-scheduling task runs; it is discarded immediately afterwards.
  • Encrypted in transit: All communication takes place over HTTPS.
  • Limited access: Only the single administrator operating 7unicorngaming can initiate tasks that touch Facebook data.

5 · Data retention

Because we do not save any Facebook-supplied data to disk or database, nothing is retained once processing completes. Log files contain only generic success/error codes and no personal content.

6 · Sharing & selling

  • We never sell your data.
  • We do not share Facebook data with any third parties or services.

7 · Your rights

If you believe we hold personal data about you, you may request a copy or deletion at any time. Simply e-mail us at cgpt.unicorn@gmail.com; we will respond within 15 days.
